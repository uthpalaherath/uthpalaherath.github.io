Hello and welcome to my personal website.
I am currently a PhD candidate in the group of Dr. Aldo Romero at the Department of Physics and Astronomy at West Virginia University. My research primarily focuses on the study of Strongly Correlated Materials (SCMs) that display intriguing properties using beyond Density Functional Theory (DFT) quantum many body methods including Dynamical Mean Field Theory (DMFT). I am also involved in code development projects that facilitate pre and post-processing of electronic structure calculations.
